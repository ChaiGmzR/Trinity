# Asset credits

The generated PNG and MP4 assets in this project were created locally for this
app as diagrammatic exercise demos.

Bundled CDC MP4 videos are public-domain works from the U.S. Centers for
Disease Control and Prevention / U.S. Department of Health and Human Services:

- Half squat: https://www.cdc.gov/physicalactivity/videos/Half_squat_IPod_Lg.mp4
- Overhead press: https://www.cdc.gov/physicalactivity/videos/Overhead_Press-Home_Ipod-Lg.mp4
- Bicep curls: https://www.cdc.gov/physicalactivity/videos/Biscep_Curls_Ipod-Lg.mp4
- Superman: https://www.cdc.gov/physicalactivity/videos/Superman_Ipod-Lg.mp4
- Sit-ups: https://www.cdc.gov/physicalactivity/videos/Sit-ups_Ipod-Lg.mp4
- Toe lift: https://www.cdc.gov/physicalactivity/videos/Toe_Lift_Ipod-Lg.mp4
